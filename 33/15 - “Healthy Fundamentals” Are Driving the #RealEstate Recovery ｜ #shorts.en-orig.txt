Real estate is a very simple business.
It's determined by supply and demand and
the cost of capital. And interestingly,
these tariffs will mean it's even more
expensive to build. So there'll be less
new supply. In addition, the cost of
capital, which went way up back in the

you've got a combination of what should
be healthy fundamentals and a sharp fall
in cost of capital. And that's what's
going to drive the real estate recovery.
