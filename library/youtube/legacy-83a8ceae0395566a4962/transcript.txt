Meet Alex. From the moment she wakes up,
she's connected to a type of capital
that runs through her everyday life.
Assetbased finance, or ABF for short.
Every ABF instrument is created with an
underlying pool of assets with
contractual cash flows like car loans,
credit cards, and mortgages. Alex begins
her day connected online. technological
infrastructure that's possible because
telecom companies turn their customers
monthly internet bills into ABF
securities, creating the capital for the
development of the cell towers and fiber
networks that provide Alex with
lightning fast connectivity. When Alex
pays her cell phone bill each month, her
payment is likewise pulled with
thousands of others and used to repay
ABF investors and generate yield on
those investments. Assetbased finance
makes so much of what Alex enjoys
possible and helps fuel investment and
growth in the world's economy.
