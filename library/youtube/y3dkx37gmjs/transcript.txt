What is an
Apple account? Your Apple account lets you
enjoy the entire
Apple ecosystem. You can access your
Apple account on all your Apple devices by
signing in with your email address
or
phone number. Your information is therefore right
where you need it, whenever you need it.
So why do you need
an Apple account? You can
sign in to your Apple account on all
your Apple devices to store and
access your personal data in
iCloud, such as your
saved passwords in the Passwords app, your
photos in the Photos app, and your
activity in the Fitness app. You can
send an unlimited number of messages
to other Apple users with High Messages
and use FaceTime to participate in
video calls, even with
Android users. You can also
sign in to compatible apps and
websites by
signing in with Apple. You can buy
apps and games in the App Store,
books in Apple Books, and
music in the iTunes Store. You can
also use Apple Music, watch
Apple TV+, and play Apple
Arcade games. And if you ever lose a
device, Find My can help you
locate it. All your Apple services are
grouped together in one account. But how do
you share your purchases
and services with loved ones? No
problem! Thanks to Family Sharing,
everyone can have their own
Apple account and share their
eligible subscriptions, purchases, and more. Your
group can even share
iCloud storage and
features like Hide My
Email and iCloud Private Relay. And
when you use Find My with
Family Sharing, if someone in
your group loses their device, you
can help them find it, even if they
're
online. Since everyone in your
group has their own Apple account, you
can share whatever you want while
keeping
your account secure and private. Your
Apple account is very important,
so protecting it is just as important.
Use a strong password,
don't reuse it elsewhere, and don't
share it with anyone. With
two-factor authentication, your
information is encrypted, your data is
protected, and no one else can log in
except
you. That's what an
Apple account is all about: one username, one password, one
account for your entire
Apple universe. To learn more about your
Apple devices, subscribe to this
