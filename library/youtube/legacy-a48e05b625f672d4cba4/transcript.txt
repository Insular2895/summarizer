Welcome to this demonstration on how
wealth management firms can align an
existing client portfolio to a model
portfolio inside MSCI wealth manager.
First, search for the client
portfolio. Next, turn on the benchmark.
In this example, a firm model portfolio.
On the bottom left, you can view the
similarity score, MSCI's proprietary
factor-based scoring metric for aligning
portfolios to models or other
benchmarks. To add a proposed portfolio,
click the plus
button, then new proposed
portfolio. Give the new portfolio a name
and click
add. This new portfolio is where the
rebalancing recommendation will be
generated. You can use the guided
rebalancing tool to add constraints
around specific holdings or asset
classes and rebalance the portfolio
based on established firm
guidelines. Here in this illustrative
example, you can see that the similarity
score improved from 59% to
91%. You can view the risk factor
attributions side by side, including
public and private assets by clicking
the gear icon. You can toggle the
benchmark on and off. Here is the model
portfolio in
purple. You can also view exposure
breakdowns by equity, geography, sector,
and
bond. This is a brief overview of MSCI
Wealth Manager client portfolio
alignment and rebalancing capabilities.
We help investment teams and advisers
streamline their portfolio management
workflows at scale. Learn more at
msei.com/wealthmanager.
